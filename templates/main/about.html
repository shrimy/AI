Welcome to House Vitalis

Modern health clinic whose mission is to provide the highest quality medical care for patients from Rzeszów and the surrounding area. For over a decade, we have been taking care of the health of our patients, offering comprehensive specialist assistance, individual approach and access to the latest treatment methods. Our clinic has been operating since 2010 and since then we have been constantly expanding our team of specialists and the scope of services to meet your health needs.

Our specializations

Cardiology

Cardiology is our leading specialization, where we focus on the diagnosis, prevention and treatment of heart and circulatory system diseases. Our team of cardiologists provides a comprehensive approach and access to modern research and technology.

Dr Jan Kowalski

Cardiology specialist with 20 years of experience. Recognized as one of the best specialists in the region.

Dr Maria Nowak

Pediatric cardiologist with many years of experience, specializing in the diagnosis of congenital heart muscle defects in the youngest patients.

Dr Michał Lewicki

Specialist in interventional cardiology, specializing in angioplasty and stenting procedures.

Endocrinology

Endocrinology is a field that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of hormonal disorders that may affect the metabolism, growth, development and functioning of many organs. Our team of endocrinologists treats, among others: diabetes, thyroid diseases and hormonal disorders.

Dr Anna Majewska

Specialist in the treatment of diabetes and hormonal disorders in adults.

Dr Ewa Kamińska

Endocrinologist specializing in diagnosing growth disorders and hormonal problems in children and adolescents.

Dr Marek Sadowski

Expert in the treatment of thyroid diseases such as Hashimoto's thyroiditis and hyperthyroidism.

Neurology

Neurology is a key specialization of our clinic, dealing with the treatment of diseases of the nervous system, such as migraine, epilepsy, degenerative diseases and multiple sclerosis. Our neurologists offer comprehensive diagnostics and therapy of neurological disorders.

Dr Katarzyna Wójcik

Specialist in the treatment of migraines and neurological disorders related to stress.

Dr Piotr Szulc

A neurologist specializing in the treatment of multiple sclerosis and degenerative diseases of the nervous system.

Dr Alicja Bielecka

Pediatric neurologist, specializing in the treatment of epilepsy and neurodevelopmental disorders in children.
